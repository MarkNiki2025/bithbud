# About the Project and Learning Process

### About Team and Project

We are developing a SaaS platform. Our team consists of the Core Team and students.  
Here you can find a video demonstration of the project: **[Project Presentation Website](https://saaskit.us/)**

### Opportunities and Tasks

**_1. Learning and Real-World Experience_**

Our team is ready to share real-world development experience.  
We invite students who want to:

-   Gain hands-on experience in commercial development and teamwork.
-   Learn version control (Git) while working in a large shared repository.
-   Understand real-world business and development processes.
-   Receive professional feedback and participate in code reviews.

**_2. Typical Tasks_**

-   Writing custom ESLint rules.
-   Refactoring and extending the main project (bug fixes, styling, utilities, tests, documentation).
-   Working with AI integration.
-   Developing and maintaining the presentation website.

### Student Path

**_1. Growth Process_**

You will learn more about each step in the following sections, but the overall path looks like this:

-   Complete the guides in this `dev-tutorials` repository.
-   Start contributing to the main repository with beginner-friendly tasks.
-   Progress from simple tasks to standard feature development.
-   Depending on current project goals, contribute to related repositories.

**_2. Prospects_**

Most students gain valuable teamwork experience and mentorship.  
Top performers may be invited to continue with a further internship as part of the Core Team.
